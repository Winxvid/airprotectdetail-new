Dry Wash

Brilliantly Clean. Remarkably Efficient. Entirely Waterless.

Brilliantly Clean. Remarkably Efficient. Entirely Waterless.

Designed for those who demand perfection, our eco-friendly, waterless solution saves valuable time while delivering unmatched results. By eliminating the need for traditional washing methods, you can reduce downtime and focus on what matters most—flying.

This innovative approach not only ensures a spotless, polished finish but also complies with environmental regulations, making it a smarter, more sustainable choice for protecting your aircraft and your investment.

Benefits

  • No rinsing required
  • Environmentally friendly and biodegradable
  • 100% streak-free finish
  • Safe for both exterior and interior surfaces
  • Sanitizes hard surfaces, including aircraft paint, metal, countertops, and polished wood
  • Removes contaminants that can damage paint
