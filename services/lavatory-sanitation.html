Lavatory & Galley Sanitation

Spotless, Hygienic, and Ready for Service

Ensure your aircraft's most critical areas meet the highest standards of cleanliness with our Lavatory & Galley Sanitation service. Using aviation-safe cleaning products, we thoroughly clean, disinfect, and deodorize these essential spaces.

From eliminating bacteria and odors to ensuring every surface is pristine, this service keeps your lavatories and galleys fresh, hygienic, and ready for passengers and crew.

Benefits

  • Thorough Disinfection: Eliminates harmful bacteria, viruses, and odors from lavatories and galleys.
  • Aviation-Safe Products: Uses cleaning solutions specifically approved for aircraft interiors.
  • Deodorized Environment: Leaves these spaces smelling fresh and clean for passenger and crew comfort.
  • Pristine Surfaces: Cleans and sanitizes every surface, from countertops to fixtures.
  • Health and Safety Focus: Promotes a hygienic environment, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Attention to Detail: Addresses all areas, including hidden spaces, to ensure a complete and thorough clean.
