About Us

Who We Are

AirProtect was founded to serve aircraft owners who want their aircraft cared for correctly, not rushed or treated like an automotive detail. As a veteran-owned company, we approach aircraft detailing with discipline, respect for materials, and an understanding that every aircraft represents a significant investment.

We specialize in preservation-first detailing using FAA-safe methods designed to protect paint, brightwork, and interior materials while minimizing unnecessary abrasion. Our focus is not chasing shine, but reducing long-term wear and maintaining value over time.
